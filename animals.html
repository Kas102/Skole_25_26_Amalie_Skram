Pygmy Hog

The pygmy hog is the smallest pig species in the world. Earlier the pygmy hog was widely spread across the Himalayas. To the current time the pygmy hog is a rare animal that can only be located in India. Standing at only 30 cm tall this animal Is a must see if you are visiting India.

Sangai Deer

The Sangai deer is a critically endangered animal that can only be found In Keibul Lamjao National Park. This animal is often referred to as the dancing deer because of its weird movement that almost looks like he dances. Sangai deer also holds a huge cultural importance. If you are traveling to India is a Animal worth the visit.

Nilgiri Tahr

The Nilgiri Tahr Is the only mountain ungulate that can be found in India. A large part of the animals is dead, and the existing population is under huge stress because of habitant loss. The animal is endangered at its truly a Animal you need to see.
