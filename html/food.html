Food

In India the food culture is different from many other countries. Indians often use a lot of ingredients such as rice and chicken, in combination with different spices and herbs. India does not have an official national dish, but khichdi is widely considered the unofficial national dish. Khichdi is a simple dish made from rice and lentils. It is enjoyed across all religions and classes.

Indian Accent

Indian accent is a great food place that is located in New Dehli, and it is recognized as one of the greatest by many people. The food there is tasty and they always have something for everybody to enjoy, whether you prefer the sweet or spicy food. The chefs there are well trained and make delicious food. The place is considered to be fancy and the price is therefore a little bit higher.

Opening hours

12:00 pm – 10:30pm Monday - Sunday


Annemaya

Annemaya is a relatively new food hall that is highly inspired by the different colors and flavors from all around the world. On the menu you will find food with all sorts of flavors and spices. You are also able to find food that is specially made for people with allergies. This restaurant is truly one of a kind , and a place that you will have to visit while travelling to India.

Opening hours

6:30 am – 12:00 am Monday - Sunday


The Table

The Table is a restaurant located in Mumbai,India. It’s a highly ranked food place that has some of the best meals in India. It has all types of meals such as lunch, dinner and even brunch. They also have special diets that are vegan or vegetarian friendly.

Opening hours

12:00 pm – 4:00 pm Monday - Saturday
