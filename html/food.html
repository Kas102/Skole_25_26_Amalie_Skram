Food

In India the food culture is different from many other countries. The food in India is made using many different spices which makes the food taste great. They also use much of ingredients such as rice and chicken that makes the food even more iconic.

Indian Accent

Indian Accent is a great food place that is located in New Dehli and is recognized as one of the greatest by many people. The food there is tasty and they always have something for every person. Either if you like sweet or spicy they have something that you will enjoy. The chef’s there are well trained and make delicious food. The place is fancy and therefore explains the higher prices. Annemaya

Annemaya is a pretty new food hall that is highly inspired by the different colors and flavors from around the world. On the menu you will find food with all sorts of flavors and spices you are even able to find food that is special made for people with allergies. This restaurant is truly on of a kind a a place that you will have to visit while travelling to India.

Opening hours

6:30 am – 12:00 am Monday - Sunday

The Table

The table is a restaurant located in Mumbai India. It’s a highly ranked food place that has some of the best meals in India. It has all types of meals like lunch, dinner and even brunch. They also have special diets that are vegan or vegetarian friendly.
